Our Family Tree

Details about the various members of our family are scattered around, in people's minds, in photos and odd documents. These pages are an attempt to pull together these snippets and provide a place future finds can be put.

Families are made up of generations, moving backwards in time. So initially the starting point is 2018, with a page that includes three generations. It is interesting to note that the middle of these three generation have, at least for the moment, decided to leave the UK and settle in other parts of the world.

There are two types of page; those that show part of the family tree, showing who is related to whom; and pages that provide some level of detail about an individual or family group.

Naturally we concentrate on the James family, but also include what we know about the Hinxman, Ludlam and Brazil families. So to view what we know of our family history, click on any of the 'families' below. You can then move from one 'family' to another.
