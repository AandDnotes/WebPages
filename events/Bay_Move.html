Angela’s Aunt’s Move

 
Angela’s Auntie Bay
decided earlier this year that living alone in Leigh-on-Sea was starting to become a concern for her. So she announced that she would move to Steyning, at the age of 95!

So she sold her house in Essex and bought a bungalow in Steyning, just ten minutes walk from our house. She brought her car with her as well, so that she could continue to drive to the shops! In fact she brought pretty much everything from a much larger house and is still trying to fit it into her new bungalow. The removal men packed everything, even a milk jug with milk in it!

So by mid September, much to our surprise, a chain of six properties all changed hands on the same day and her bulky furniture plus 70 boxes of belongings were installed in Steyning.

Luckily the bungalow was in excellent condition and so she could move straight in. However, we are still helping her unpack boxes. One room, at the far end of the bungalow, has become the store room.
