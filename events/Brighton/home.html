A Day Out in Brighton

Our friends Kate and Dick visited us for a long weekend in the spring of 2012 and on Monday 2nd April we took the Coastliner bus from Shoreham (we all have bus passes) into Brighton. Naturally we walked along the pier and took a spin on the Brighton Wheel, but we also walked along the promenade to the marina for lunch (and sat outside). We then caught the Volke's railway back to Madiera Drive, toured the Pavilion and walked around the Lanes. Here are some of our postcards.
