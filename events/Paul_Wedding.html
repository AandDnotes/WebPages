Paul and Krista’s Wedding

 
 
 

Date: 13th November 2010

Venue: Pembroke Lodge, Richmond Park, Surrey, UK

Krista and Paul have known each other for about eight years, have toured Australia and New Zealand together and now live in Twickenham. Krista comes from a small village just outside Rotterdam, but decided to get married in the UK since most of their friends are here. Her family and Dutch friends came over for the ceremony and reception, both of which were held in Pembroke Lodge in the middle of Richmond Park.
