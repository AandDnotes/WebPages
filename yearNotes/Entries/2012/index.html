2012

At the beginning of April some friends visited us fro the weekend and we had a day out in Brighton. Since we all have bus passes, we caught the 700 Coastliner from Shoreham and spent an enjoyable (and sunny) day in Brighton. You will find a postcard from Brighton here.

Then at the beginning of May we became grandparents! Victoria was due to give birth about two weeks before Krista, but Victoria was late and Krista was early, so Rowan arrived three days before Austin. Photos of them both can be found here.

June saw the Queen's Diamond Jubilee and we had a street party in the Close. We made this short YouTube video. June also saw major work relaying our drains!

In July we visited the freinds who were with us in Brighton and at the beginning of October we had a few days in Cambridge. Needless to say, most of our short trips were to Twickenham to visit Rowan and to Binfield to see Austin.
