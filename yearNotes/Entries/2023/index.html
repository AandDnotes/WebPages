2023

Looking back, there were quite a lot of trips/outings this year. We went to Alan’s twice, once for Christine’s birthday and then later for a halloween party. During the first trip we also went on to meet up with Kate and Dick for lunch. We then met them again, this time for a few days, in Bury St Edmunds in September.

Angela met up with her friends in Guildford and we spent a week with Paul et al in Berkenwoude, where we went again for Christmas.

However, our biggest trip was to visit Victoria et al in New Jersey, where they had moved in early 2021. We started by flying to Boston for a few days and then caught the train down to New York and then out to New Jersey. The whole trip was very good (apart from picking up a heavy cold on the train from Boston), although we found parts of New Jersey too hectic.
