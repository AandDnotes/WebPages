James Edward James

Born 19th November 1923 at home at 19 Loanda Street, Haggerston, Shorditch.

Married to Eileen on Saturday 6th February 1943 at St James's Church in Fore Street, Edmonton

Read Jim's notes on his life. These notes cover his childhood, time spent in the Army in India and some of his jobs.

This is the earliest photo that we have of Jim, here with his younger brother Mick. Jim must be nine or ten years old, so the photo was taken in about 1932.

Moving forward 14 years to 1946 we find Jim in the Army in India.

The remaining three photos have no dates or locations, but the first shows four Seargents.
