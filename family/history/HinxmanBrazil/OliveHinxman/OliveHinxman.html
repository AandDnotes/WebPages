Olive Lois Hinxman

Born 31st December 1910.

Married to John on 8th June 1940.

Died 2011.

The earlist photo we have of Olive is this one with her mother, Lois, and younger sister, Gertrude, in 1917. The same three are seen a little later in a station waiting room. The original of this photo is a glass plate negative.
