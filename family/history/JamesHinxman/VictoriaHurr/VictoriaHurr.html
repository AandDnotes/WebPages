Victoria Ann Hurr (nee James)

Born 11th December 1976 in Warrington hospital, Cheshire.

Early years in Lymm, Cheshire, before moving in 1990 to Bramber, West Sussex.

Obtained a BSc in molecular biology from UMIST (before it merged with the University of Manchester) and then a PhD from the John Innes Centre in Norwich. Then became a post-doc at the University of Florida.

Married Brandon Hurr in Florida on 13th January 2007.
