At the beginning of September we visited Italy. We flew from Gatwick on Tuesday to Rome and then went by coach to a small spa town called Chianciano. This was really just a base from which to visit Sienna and Florence. We then went via Assisi to Rome. Our 'tour manager', who we met at Rome airport, was called Dotty. She was a young(ish) German woman who now lives in Rome and spoke English without a German accent.
