Madeira 2011

 

During the first week of April we had a short break in Madeira. These pages give a mainly pictorial view of what we found.

 
 

Madeira is the main island of a small archipelago in the Atlantic, north of the Canary Islands. It is part of Portugal. We stayed just to the west of the capital city, about a half hour walk from the centre.
