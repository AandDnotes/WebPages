Hotel Vila Vicencia

 

Vila Vincencia is three houses that have been converted into a hotel, set within tropical gardens and completely secluded from the surrounding buildings. The entrance is via a key through a gate on a side road. Even then it is only two minutes walk to the main shopping road and five minutes walk from the sea.
